1-Mohamed Salah - The Egyptian King

Mohamed Salah has become one of Liverpool's all-time greats in a short period. Since joining the club in 2017, Salah has scored

over 180 goals and has played a vital role in Liverpool's recent successes, including their Champions League triumph in 2019 and

Premier League title in 2020. Salah's ability to score consistently has made him a fan favorite.

2-Kenny Dalglish - The King of Anfield

Kenny Dalglish is regarded as Liverpool FC's greatest player of all time. Known as "King Kenny," Dalglish was a crucial figure in

the club's most successful periods in the 1970s and 1980s. As both a player and manager, he led Liverpool to multiple league

titles and European Cups, forever engraving his name in the club's history.

3-Steven Gerrard - The Ultimate Captain

Steven Gerrard is often seen as Liverpool's greatest player for his leadership and ability to single-handedly carry the team

through tough times. A true homegrown talent, Gerrard played for Liverpool from 1998 to 2015, scoring 186 goals in 710

appearances. He is remembered for his incredible goals, especially in crucial matches like the 2005 Champions League final and

the 2006 FA Cup final.

4-Luis Suarez - The Controversial Genius

Luis Suarez was one of the most technically gifted players to play for Liverpool. The Uruguayan striker joined the club in 2011

and quickly became a fan favorite due to his incredible skill and tenacity. Suarez scored 82 goals in 133 appearances and was a

key player in Liverpool's near-title-winning campaign in the 2013-2014 season.

5-Virgil van Dijk - The Defensive Colossus

Virgil van Dijk transformed Liverpool's defense after joining the club in 2018. Known for his leadership and defensive skills, Van

Dijk played a significant role in Liverpool's recent successes, including their Champions League win in 2019 and Premier League

title in 2020. His presence at the back has made Liverpool one of the toughest teams to break down.
